WHO WE ARE

SHIELD Society or Software Hardware Integration Engineering Leaders And Developers Society is one of the student organizations of Cavite State University - Carmona Campus bearing the flag of computer engineering students.

OUR UPBRINGING

We are democratically autonomous, duly recognized, and representative student organization whose objective is to create an environment of learning, collaboration, and innovation among students, faculty, and staff, guided by CvSU's core values. We aim to excel academically, bridge theory and practice, and uphold integrity while dedicating ourselves to serving our community
